Easy Lasagna

Description

A creamy, savory and delicious dish to make for dinners and meals. Incredibly filling, and is probably the best comfort food there is out there.

Having a bad day? Make some lasagna and settle in with your favorite movie! It is sure to cozy you up and make you feel better!

Ingredients

  • Beef; you can use ground turkey for a lighter option
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: cottage cheese, mozzarella and Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings: dried parsley, salt, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles (of course!)
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna : Layer the ingredients, in order- meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, Parmesan cheese- and repeat until finished. Top with remaining Parmesan
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
